## 🎯 How to Use Dark Mode

### Desktop
1. Look at the top navbar
2. Find the toggle switch (between navigation and "Sign in")
3. Click to switch between light ☀️ and dark 🌙 mode
4. Your choice is automatically saved

### Mobile
1. Tap the hamburger menu (☰)
2. Look for "Theme" section
3. Toggle between light and dark mode
4. Close menu to see the change

### Features
- **Remembers your choice** — Saved in browser
- **System preference** — Respects your OS setting
- **Smooth transition** — No jarring flashes
- **All components** — Everything switches seamlessly
